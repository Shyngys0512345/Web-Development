Beshbarmaq

  • Time: 2 hours 20 minutes(overall)
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
lamb or beef, cubed 1 kg
wide flat noodles (usually homemade) 500g
potatoes, peeled and quartered 5-6
onions, finely chopped 3
Salt and pepper to taste optional
Fresh herb optional

Step 1: In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the cubed meat and cook until tender, skimming off any foam that rises to the top.

Step 2: Once the meat is cooked, remove it from the broth and set it aside. Keep the broth for cooking the noodles.

Step 3: In the same pot, bring the broth back to a boil and add the quartered potatoes. Cook until the potatoes are tender.

Step 4: Cook the wide flat noodles in the meat broth until they are done. Drain the noodles.

Step 5: Arrange the boiled meat, potatoes, and noodles on a large serving platter.

Step 6: In a separate pan, sauté the chopped onions until golden brown. Spread the sautéed onions over the meat, potatoes, and noodles.

Step 7: Serve hot, allowing guests to use their hands to enjoy the dish, as is the tradition with Beshbarmak.

Quyrdaq

  • Time: 1 hour 5 minutes(overall)
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
lamb or beef liver, sliced 500g
lamb or beef heart, sliced 500g
lamb or beef kidney, sliced 500g
large onions, finely chopped 2
potatoes, peeled and diced 4-5
carrots, peeled and sliced 3-4
tablespoons vegetable oil 3
Salt and pepper to taste optional
Fresh herbs for garnish optional

Step 1: In a large skillet or wok, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.

Step 2: Add the sliced liver, heart, and kidney to the skillet. Cook until browned on all sides.

Step 3: Remove the browned meats from the skillet and set them aside.

Step 4: In the same skillet, add chopped onions and cook until translucent.

Step 5: Add diced potatoes and sliced carrots to the skillet. Cook until vegetables start to soften.

Step 6: Return the browned meats to the skillet and mix well with the vegetables.

Step 7: Season the mixture with salt and pepper according to taste.

Step 8: Cover the skillet and let the ingredients simmer on low heat until the vegetables are fully cooked and the flavors are well combined.

Step 9: Garnish with fresh herbs before serving.

Source: Quyrdaq recipe website



Bauyrsaq

  • Time: 1 hour(overall)
  • Serves: 8-10
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flour 4 cups
warm water 1 cup
salt 1 tsp
sugar 1 tbsp
vegetable oil 1 tbsp
oil for deep frying

Step 1: Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit until frothy.

Step 2: Combine flour, salt, yeast mixture, and oil to form dough.

Step 3: Let dough rise for 30-40 mins.

Step 4: Shape dough into balls, flatten, and twist.

Step 5: Deep fry until golden brown.

Step 6: Serve warm with tea, honey, or jam.
